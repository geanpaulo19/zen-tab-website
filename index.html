Philosophy

A tab that asks nothing of you
except your attention.

Most productivity tools add friction. They ask you to log in, configure projects, organize hierarchies. They compete for your focus before you've even started work.

Zen New Tab is the opposite. It appears in a moment of transition, when you open a new tab, and asks only one question: what matters right now?

The clock grounds you in the present. The quote sets a tone. The background gives your eyes somewhere calm to rest. Everything else is optional, draggable, dismissible. Yours.

✦

Minimal by design

Every pixel earns its place. Nothing decorative without purpose.

◈

Private by default

No accounts, no telemetry, no ads. All data lives in your browser.

⊹

Yours to shape

Toggle every feature on or off. Drag widgets anywhere. Your tab, your rules.
